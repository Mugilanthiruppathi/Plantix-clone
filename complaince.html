How are you protected against retalition?

Rule#1


Our clear commitment



We do not tolerate any discrimination,

unequal treatment, intimidation, or other

negative effects for the person providing

the information. If the company

becomes aware of such an act within its

sphere of influence, it will take

appropriate measures.

Confidentiality



One of the main reasons for setting up a

whistleblower system is to protect both

the person making the report and the

accused. Therefore, all information

relevant in connection with the

processing of tips must be treated as

strictly confidential.
